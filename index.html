About

Let us introduce ourselves

Civil Arcade, a company incorporated under The Registration of Firms,Hyderabad (South) prescribed by Section 58(1) of the Indian Partnership Act,1932., in the year 2016.

We started with one service of providing assistance to Civil Engineering Students in Competitive & Univeristy Examinations. After successfully providing Educational Assitance for more than One(1) year we added few more services to our company and started growing thereafter.

Civil Arcade is founded by Four(4) Directors, who holds Masters Degree. We are very proud company to be recognised as "Startup" by the Government of India under Make in India Initiative. We are the first company to be recognised in South India under Education Sector.

Specializations: Structural Engineering, Construction Management, Regional & Urban Planning
